PulseGuard Product & Regulatory Guidelines:

Claims SLA: Under the Kenya Insurance (Index Insurance) Regulations 2025, all weather-related parametric claims must be settled automatically within 10 days of the triggering event.

Pricing: Micro-policies must be priced affordably, with minimum premiums strictly starting at KSh 50 per week for Boda-Boda personal accident cover.

Boda-Boda Coverage: The standard Boda-Boda medical cover provides hospitalization benefits up to KSh 100,000.

Mutual Pools: Community chamas can form mutual pools; if claims are low, surplus is returned as a 10% premium rebate at the end of the year.
